About Me

I am an Assistant Prof. in School of Mathematical Sciences, South China Normal University, Guangzhou, China.

Research Interests

  • Mathematical Competitions
  • Mathematical Identity
  • Gifted Education
  • Growth and Development of Outstanding Mathematicians

Current Research

Drawing upon life course theory and self-determination theory, my doctoral dissertation utilizes curriculum vitae (CV) analysis and in-depth interviews to investigate the developmental trajectories of 23 Chinese outstanding young mathematicians.
